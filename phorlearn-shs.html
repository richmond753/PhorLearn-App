📐 Elective Maths · SHS 3 · WASSCE Syllabus
Integration — The Definite Integral
Understand area under a curve, limits of integration, and real-world applications.
Lesson Content

Definition
The definite integral ∫ₐᵇ f(x) dx gives the net area between the curve f(x) and the x-axis, from x = a to x = b.


Key Rule (Power Rule)
∫ xⁿ dx = xⁿ⁺¹ / (n+1) + C, where n ≠ −1


Worked Example
Find ∫₁³ (2x + 1) dx
= [x² + x]₁³ = (9+3) − (1+1) = 12 − 2 = 10
